About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Sydney Colledge! I am so happy that you found yourself to my page! Before I jump right in to my design work, I want to tell you a little about myself.

I am 23 years old and I am currently a Graphic Design student at UVU. I was born in Sydney, Australia and raised in northern Utah for most of my life. As a kid, I was always an athlete and never considered myself to be creative or artsy. However, I had many others in my family who were that way. In my mind, I could only be one thing and that was an athlete. I would take art classes in school and truly enjoyed them. I would do small art projects here and there too. I always knew I was creative but would supress that part of me until I was older. I committed to play volleyball at some colleges and did that for a few years. Throughout college is where I started exploring my creative side, when I had time away from volleyball of course.

A few creative things that I have tried;

  • Cermics/Pottery
  • Water Color Painting
  • Bullet Journaling
  • Social Media/Content Creation
  • Video/Photo Editing
  • Photography
  • Graphic Design for Work/Social Media

I started designing on Adobe Illustrator and Procreate on my own. I would do small projects that I made up or a project that a close friend or family member asked me to do. I finally landed on Graphic Design as my degree after realizing that it was my passion. The plan for the future is to finish my degree at UVU while I create and design on the side for small clients. My goal is to start my own design business where I can set my own hours, work for myself, and work from anywhere in the world.
